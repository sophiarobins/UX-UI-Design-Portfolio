About

Designer who
ships things.

I'm Sophia Robins — a UX/UI designer and developer based in the Pacific Northwest, currently finishing my final semester at Western Governors University where I'm completing a Bachelor's degree in UX and UI Design, graduating Fall 2026.

I also run Chasing Robins Media, a wedding photography business I built from the ground up. Along the way I discovered that the tools available to photographers were either too generic, too expensive, or completely blind to the client's experience — so I designed and built my own. A full-stack web application, from brand identity to deployed product, while finishing school.

That's how I work. I don't just design interfaces — I understand the business problem behind them, the user feeling in front of them, and the code connecting them underneath. I'm actively looking for full-time UX/UI design roles to step into after graduation, and available for freelance work now.
